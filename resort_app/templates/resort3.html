About Us

The property nests over a sea of trees that stretch as far as
the eye can see. From the resort, treetops of palm and many
kinds of trees fill the view, making the vista a collage of
rich greens. Just a little bit over the edge of the tree line,
the view opens up to the sea with the faint silhouette of Bohol
Island – what a sight.
